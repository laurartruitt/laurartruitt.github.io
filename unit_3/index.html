About Our Breads

Long development helps this naturally fermented loaf improve with age. We take great pride in creating these handcrafted Our breads and pastry are baked daily on site in Morristown, NJ. Sourdough loaves have a cold slow rise overnight before being baked off each morning. Sourdoughs take time to make. That three simple ingredients - flour, water, and salt - can produce such amazing results is truly a wonder.
